Select the appropriate cruise altitude for a eastbound IFR aircraft.
A.	10,000
B.	10,500
C.	11,000
D.	11,500
ANSWER: C

Select the appropriate cruise altitude for a westbound VFR aircraft. 
A.	10,000
B.	10,500
C.	11,000
D.	11,500
ANSWER: B

B.	Departure Frequency
C.	All of the Above
